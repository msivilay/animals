Mammals

Mammals are so abundant on this earth; they are what we typically think of when we hear the word "animal." The definition of a mammal is that it an organism that is of the kingdom animalia and have mammary glands.  top



Marsupials

Marsupials are mammals of an order whose members are born incompletely developed and are typically carried and suckled in a pouch on the mother's belly. Many of the world's cutest animals, in my opinion, are marsupials.  top



Reptiles

Reptiles are vertebrate animals of a class that includes snakes, lizards, crocodiles, turtles, and tortoises. They are distinguished by having a dry scaly skin and typically laying soft-shelled eggs on land.  top
